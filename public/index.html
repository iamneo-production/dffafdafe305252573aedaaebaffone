Who we are?

Charity is a nonprofit that supports other nonprofits by connecting them to donors and companies. We are a dedicated nonprofit bridging communities and causes, fostering positive change globally. With a legacy since 2000, we empower grassroots organizations with resources, training, and support. Our crowdfunding platform connects donors and changemakers, enabling impactful projects to thrive. Join us in a shared journey of compassion, making a lasting difference worldwide. Together, we champion transformative initiatives that shape a better future for all. We're a committed nonprofit uniting causes, empowering local groups with resources since 2000. Our platform connects donors and initiatives, fostering global change.
